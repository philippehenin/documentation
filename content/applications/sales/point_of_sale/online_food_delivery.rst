Online Food Delivery
====================

Online food delivery allows restaurants to receive and manage customer orders placed through various delivery platforms, streamlining the order process. With online food delivery integration, businesses can reach a larger customer base and increase operational efficiency.

.. note::
   Ensure you have the required credentials from the delivery platform to complete the integration.
